For this project, I created a Python script to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. I utilized the OpenWeatherMap API to create a representative model of weather across world cities.

During the project I created scatter plot visualizations to showcase the following relationships:

* Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
* Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
* Maximum Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
* Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude

You can find these visualizations by clicking on the pictures to the right side of the screen, or in the dropdown menu, where you will also find links to a side-by-side comparison and some of the raw city data.
